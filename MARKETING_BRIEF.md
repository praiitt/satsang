## Satsang App – Marketing Strategy Brief

This brief equips the marketing team with positioning, audiences, messages, channels, and KPIs to plan and execute growth for RRAASI सत्संग.

## Product overview

- RRAASI सत्संग: a voice‑first spiritual companion in Hindi. Users talk to “Guruji” to get answers, hear pravachan, play bhajans, and receive guidance.
- Delivery: mobile‑first web app (PWA). No install required. 15‑minute free trial. Fast phone‑auth sign‑in.
- Core experience: speak in Hindi → real‑time response → optional bhajan/pravachan playback → invites to ongoing “sत्संग”.

## Value proposition

- “Your spiritual guide, in your language, on demand.”
- Benefits:
  - Instant guidance in natural Hindi speech
  - Effortless bhajan/pravachan playback and discovery
  - Private, judgment‑free questions
  - Available anytime, anywhere (mobile browser)

## Target audiences and personas

- Primary: Hindi‑first users 25–55 in India who practice bhakti; daily commuters; homemakers; students seeking calm/discipline.
- Secondary:
  - NRI diaspora 30–60 (Hindi spiritual content)
  - Seniors 55+ (low friction, voice‑first)
  - Devotional music listeners on YouTube/Spotify
- B2B2C partners: Mandirs, kirtan groups, satsang communities, spiritual influencers.

## Top use cases

- Morning/evening arati companion; daily shloka; guided japa timer
- Quick spiritual questions in Hindi
- “Play [deity] bhajan” while working or commuting
- Topic‑based pravachan queue (“भक्ति पर प्रवचन सुनाओ”)

## Differentiators

- Native Hindi voice experience end‑to‑end
- Hands‑free, speech‑first interaction
- Immediate music/pravachan playback and discovery
- No app install; 30 seconds to first value

## Messaging pillars (Hindi/English)

- Guidance: “आध्यात्मिक प्रश्नों के सीधे उत्तर — हिंदी में, तुरंत।” / “Get spiritual answers, instantly in Hindi.”
- Devotion: “भजन और प्रवचन, बस बोलिए।” / “Bhajans and pravachans, just say it.”
- Simplicity: “बिना ऐप — सीधे ब्राउज़र में।” / “No app, just your browser.”
- Safety: “निजी, सुरक्षित, सदैव उपलब्ध।” / “Private, safe, always there.”

## Elevator pitch

RRAASI सत्संग is an AI‑powered Hindi spiritual companion. Speak naturally to Guruji, get guidance, listen to bhajans and pravachans, and deepen your practice—without installing an app.

## Landing page structure (mobile‑first)

- H1: “आपका आध्यात्मिक गुरु — अब हिंदी में”
- Subhead: Benefit‑driven two lines + trust badges (trial, privacy)
- Demo video (tap‑to‑play, not auto‑playing with sound)
- 3 value cards: Guidance, Bhajans, Pravachan
- Social proof: testimonials, partner logos, usage counter
- CTA: “गुरुजी से बातचीत करें” (+ 15‑min free trial note)
- FAQ, Footer (Privacy, Terms, Contact)

## Pricing and packaging (proposal)

- Free: 15 minutes/day, limited playback length
- Plus: Monthly subscription (INR 99–199): longer sessions, playlists, daily routines, “Add to Home” prompt
- Family/Temple plan for shared devices at mandirs

## Acquisition channels

- Organic:
  - YouTube Shorts/Reels (20–30s): “Ask Guruji” clips; daily bhajan snippets
  - SEO: Hindi queries (“भक्ति क्या है?”, “सद्गुरु का प्रवचन”) topic hubs
  - WhatsApp share flows from within app
- Paid:
  - Meta/YouTube targeting bhakti interest clusters and Hindi locales
  - Search ads on Hindi/hinglish queries (brand + non‑brand)
- Partnerships:
  - Temple QR codes, satsang groups, devotional musicians, Hindi podcasters
  - Festival activations (Janmashtami, Navratri, Diwali, etc.)
- Influencer co‑creation:
  - Bhajan singers record “call‑and‑response” with Guruji
  - “Daily Shloka with meaning” short series

## Activation funnel

Impression → Landing → Tap Demo → Phone sign‑in → Mic permission → 1st successful response → Share to WhatsApp or “Add to Home” PWA prompt.

Friction removers: clear mic instruction prompt, one‑tap retry, “What can I say?” hints.

## Retention and lifecycle

- Day 0: WhatsApp/SMS with 3 suggested prompts in Hindi
- Day 1–7: Daily reminder at chosen time (opt‑in); “Today’s shloka/bhajan”
- Weekly: “Your week in satsang” digest with listened topics and recommendations
- Re‑engagement: Festival‑specific flows, daily practice streaks

## Core metrics and goals

- Acquisition: CTR, CVR to sign‑in, CPA
- Activation: Mic permission rate, time‑to‑first‑voice (TTFV), first‑success rate
- Engagement: Sessions/user/week, avg session length, content starts
- Retention: D1/D7/D30 retention
- Monetization: Trial→paid conversion, churn, LTV
- Quality: CSAT for responses, content completion rates

## Experiment backlog (A/B)

- H1 variants (guidance vs devotion lead)
- First‑prompt suggestions: 3 vs 5; bhajan‑first vs question‑first
- CTA color and placement
- Reminder timing and tone
- Trial length 10 vs 15 mins

## Content plan (first 6 weeks)

- Daily: 1 short (YouTube/IG/FB) in Hindi with caption and CTA link
- 3× weekly: “Ask Guruji” Q&A short
- 2× weekly: Bhajan micro‑performances with on‑screen lyrics
- Weekly: 5–8 min pravachan highlight
- Blog: Hindi explainers; glossary of bhakti terms; festival guides

## Creative guidelines

- Tone: respectful, warm, non‑dogmatic, inclusive across sampradayas
- Visuals: saffron/cream palette (aligns with app theme), clean serif/sans
- Always include Hindi first; English subtitles optional
- Mobile‑first compositions; large captions; clear CTAs

## Trust, safety, and compliance

- Prominent Privacy/Terms; “for spiritual guidance only” (no medical/financial advice)
- Curated content from known teachers; licensed bhajans where required
- Clear opt‑in for notifications; simple opt‑out

## Launch plan (suggested)

- Week 0: Creative set, landing ready, analytics events mapped (sign‑in, mic grant, first utterance, first playback)
- Week 1: Soft launch to WhatsApp groups and partner mandirs
- Week 2: Influencer collabs; paid test (Rs 10–20k); iterate on creatives
- Week 3: Festival tie‑in; PR note to Hindi media
- Week 4: Expand geo targeting; referral incentives

## Sample creatives (Hindi‑first)

- Hooks
  - “आपका प्रश्न—गुरुजी का उत्तर, अभी.”
  - “कहिए ‘कृष्ण का भजन चलाओ’—और आनंद लीजिए.”
- Ad copy (30–60 chars)
  - “गुरुजी से बात करें—हिंदी में, तुरंत.”
  - “भजन/प्रवचन, बस बोलिए. न ऐप, न झंझट.”
- CTA
  - “अभी आज़माएँ (15 मिनट मुफ्त)”

## Lifecycle templates

- Welcome (WhatsApp/SMS): “नमस्ते! आज़माएँ ‘भक्ति क्या है?’ या ‘हरे कृष्ण चलाओ’. 15 मिनट मुफ्त—बस बोलिए.”
- Day 1 nudge: “आज की श्लोक प्रेरणा: [श्लोक]. कहिए ‘आज का श्लोक सुनाओ’.”
- Win‑back (Day 7): “आइए फिर जुड़ें—‘सद्गुरु का प्रवचन सुनाओ’ कहकर शुरू करें.”

## FAQ (for marketing use)

- What to promise? Instant voice answers in Hindi + bhajans/pravachans; 15‑min free trial.
- Device support? Modern mobile browsers; no install.
- Data use? Minimal PII; private; opt‑in only.

## Assets needed from marketing

- 8–10 short vertical videos (hooks above)
- Logo variants on saffron/cream
- Partner logos and 5 testimonials
- Festival template pack (Navratri/Diwali/etc.)

## Analytics events to track (naming suggestion)

- auth_start, auth_success
- mic_prompt_shown, mic_granted, mic_denied
- first_utterance, response_success, response_error
- content_start (type: bhajan/pravachan), content_complete
- cta_click (placement), share_whatsapp, pwa_add_to_home
- subscription_view, subscription_start, subscription_success, subscription_cancel
